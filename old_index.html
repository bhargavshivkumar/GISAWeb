About Us:

Graduate Indian Student Association (GISA) is a non-profit organization recognized by the Graduate Students Association at the University at Buffalo.

University at Buffalo is one of the campuses in the State University of New York system – the SUNY schools. We welcome you to take advantage of UB’s great diversity and large Indian population and participate in the various cultural events, gatherings and parties throughout the year.

We represent around 900 students. GISA activities include organizing get-togethers and events to bring together the Indian Community and share our proud values. We also give a hand to the incoming students from India by providing temporary accommodation. We strive to make their stay in the new place as comfortable as possible.

We aim at providing a friendly and pleasant atmosphere to the Indian student community. We promote the awareness of cultural diversity on campus by organizing various cultural and social events.

Please browse through the information and feel free to offer your comments / suggestions.
